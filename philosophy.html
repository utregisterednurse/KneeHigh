KNEE HIGH CHILD CARE PHILOSOPHY

As the owner of Knee High Child Care, I strive daily to provide a place where children are encouraged and loved. I truly believe that children thrive when they’re happy and secure and so our days are spent learning and playing, both together and individually. Research proves that children do their best learning when they have a balance between structured activities and self-guided activities, or as we call it, between teacher led learning activities and free play time. Here they’ll receive teacher guided learning every morning, including Language skills, Music, S.T.E.M. (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) activities which may look like collecting bugs and looking at them, learning about their life cycles, freezing ice cubes, learning to count by making butter, making buildings out of boxes, etc). Art and Dramatic Play. Study also shows that the development of social skills is the most important indicator of later career and life success, therefore, I place high importance on working on their social skills. We also follow the County Guide for Kindergarten Preparedness so that when that time comes they’ll be ready to move on to ‘Big Kid School’. We place a very high importance on learning to play and work together, caring for each other, being outside in nature as much as possible, working through our adversities, and celebrating our successes, which may look like typical playground activities, caring for our pets, working and learning about things in our garden, and plain old having fun together. It all adds up to a full day of learning and playing with our friends.
